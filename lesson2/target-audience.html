Target Audience

Mi Clima App is designed to be used by people of all ages, backgrounds and interests. Our goal is to serve people who want reliable forecast information in the cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Personas

Carlitos Pérez

Carlitos is 46 years old and loves to travel throughout the country. He has traveled since he was a child, and it is important for him to know the climate of the wasteland city to plan his adventure. He usually uses his notebook to carry out his investigations, he needs clear and complete information.

Roberto Rodriguéz

Roberto travels to different cities for work and needs to know the weather before traveling in case he must replan his plan in case of snowstorms. He uses his cell phone to check the weather information.

Scenarios

  • I'm an adventurer and I want to know the weather to go on vacation.
  • I work in different cities and want to know the weather.
  • How hotest or coldest will it be tomorrow?
  • When will it rain/snow and for how long?
  • Is the information clear for any age group?
