Site Plan

Site Name

Lomas de Zamora Chamber

Site Purpose

The primary purpose of this site is to provide business owners an opportunity to connect with others to help grow the community. The chamber site will provide important information such as people to contact in the chamber, benefits of becoming a member, events, as well as demographic and historical information about the Lomas de Zamora city. It is a small town but has quite a few attractions for those visiting either for the summer or to visit families such as Santa Catalina Municipal Nature Reserve and rich city history. A goal of the site will be to also welcome and invite new businesses to come help grow and develop the town and provide retail and dining options for the community.
