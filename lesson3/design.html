Color Scheme

The primary color will be used on features such as the navbar, footer, and buttons as well as the logo.

Secondary color will be used for some text, bottons, and design features.

Accents will be used for design features. Most text will be in black.

Typography

DM Sans

DM Sans is a sans serif that looks good in all sizes and thicknesses. This font will be used for paragraphs, buttons, and the navbar.

Ubuntu

This font will be used for headings.
