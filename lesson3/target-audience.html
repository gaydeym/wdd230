Target Audience

The target audience for this site is those within the age range of 30-65 years old and have experience in business ownership. Website visitors would be local or new business owners wanting to contribute to the quality of life of the community members to grow their business and connect with other business owners in the area.

Personas

Carlitos Pérez: Experienced Business Owner

Carlitos is 46 years old and the CEO of a retail store. She has been the CEO for the past 7 years. She is currently planning on building a store in the Lomas de Zamora area and wants to connect with other business owners in the area and understand the town's demographics better.

Roberto Rodriguéz

Roberto is a Lomas de Zamora local and has lived here his whole life. He is 53 years old and is the current owner of his family's repair shop. He wants to continue growing his business and connections within the area. He is also Native Latin and wishes to work with the community in preserving the history of Lomas de Zamora. He doesn't do much business on his phone and prefers using a desktop or his Dell laptop.

Scenarios

  • I am a local business owner looking to join the chamber.
  • I am an owner of a business in a nearby town and would like to bring my business to Lomas de Zamora city.
  • I am a member of the chamber and have a complaint/suggestion.
  • Is there room for growth in Lomas de Zamora?
  • When is the next event?
  • When is the next payment due for members of the chamber?
  • What is in or around the Lomas de Zamora are that attracts consumers?
